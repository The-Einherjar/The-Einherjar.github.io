Reptile Man V: The Destiny of The Talismans

Chapter 1

Sirens were all over the city of Lynnwood. There was another criminal on the loose. “Ready, Garry?” Jake asked. Troy nodded his head and they took off. The man wore a dark jacket , he sported blades. He gripped one. Jake camouflaged. “Right.” Garry said, aiming a grappler. Jake turned visible again and punched the ruffian, who ricocheted back up. Garry shot the grappler, the man cut the line with his blade. He then took another knife. Garry unfastened his bow. The rogue hurled the blades, one at Garry one at Jake. Garry shot a laser arrow, knocking the blade out the air. Jake leaped, the knife missed. “You are all getting on my nerves!” The rogue shouted. “They are not the only ones.” A voice said. An insect-like creature with claws dropped down and thumped the rogue on the head. The ruffian fell to the floor, out for the count. “Ino!” Jake exclaimed. “That is correct.” The Inster said. “A lot of things have happened since we last saw you.” Garry said. “Indeed, chasing dozens of criminals, practically night and day, fighting against a Firemancer. I think you are all doing great.” Ino said.

They were at the Gibbons. “Ino, what have you been doing all this time?” Jill asked. “I was keeping watch at Bamtou…and studying the Plague of Zarakqua.” Ino said. “The plague? You mean the thing that Jake, Trevor, Ryan, and Brandon have to stop the thing that goes:

One Talisman For Fire Which Heats The Good Side Higher One Talisman For Aqua To Defeat The Plague Called Zarakqua One Talisman For Plants To Protect The Earth From Evil Hands One Talisman For Reptiles Forged In The Great Tiles Four Talismans To Banish Them All Four Talismans To Seal Them In Prison Walls Four Talismans To Save The Earth

And On Flameritilquaoak’s Eve Stop The Plague’s rebirth?” Jill asked. “Yes, that is indeed exactly what I am talking about.” Ino said. There was a sizeable clamor outside. “What was that noise?” Jill asked. “What noise?” Trevor, who was sleepy, asked. “That strange roaring sound outside.” Jill replied. “It’s too big and strange to be a truck.” Ryan said. “Let’s go check it out.” Brandon said as they all turned into their talisman forms.
