The Creature of Ancient Destruction

Chapter 16

They were at the Gibbons, this would be one of the last times they would be. “I cannot conceive of you moving.” Trevor uttered. “I know.” Brianna replied. “If you have need of help, call us. We’ll come.” Mr. Gibbons said. “You call us likewise, you know, if some ancient villain comes for revenge or something.” Jake answered. “We will.” Mr. Gibbons said, shaking Jake’s hand. ”Where will you go, Garry?” Ryan asked. “I’m heading out to the eastern states. It’s where my father was born.” Garry responded. “We’re going to miss you.” Jake said. ”We’ll miss you and this, too.” Jill replied. There was a siren in the distance. ”Shall we go for one last adventure?” Brandon asked. Garry grasped his bow, Jill transformed. Jake, Ryan, Brandon, and Trevor changed. “Did you really have to ask?” Jill asked. Repti leaped in the direction of the siren and roared.
