It was the night, the night of the beginning of what they decided to call the Hunt of the Talismans. Jake and made it to the Gibbons’ house. Everyone who was fighting Gost were there. People who know about him now call him Grower.” Ino said. “What is he?” Jill, who came into the conversation asked. “He is a Crypt, a lost one. He is the only survivor of the Crypts of Rorzron.” Ino said. “Rorzron?” Jill asked, “Realm of Fire.
