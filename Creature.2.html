The Creature of Ancient Destruction

Chapter 2

“You failed?” A figure roared, he had a purple dagger. “Apologies, chief. They were too much for us.” One of the ninjas said. “You weaklings! I guess I’ll have to send my hunters.” The cloaked person said.

Jake was walking back and forth in his neighborhood. He was thinking and waiting. They were going to have a meeting there. His neighbor, Min Su came toward him. “Hey Jake, what are you doing?” He asked. He had a blue and gray staff, with blades on each side. “Just waiting.” Jake replied. “For what?” Min Su asked. Without warning, three cloaked figures with spears, daggers, and swords, were riding towards them. “Not again.” Min Su he turned into a blue diamond creature. He shot a blue beam, the blast smashed into a rock in front of an assailant. The invaders bolted. “What just happened?” Jake asked. “They were assasins sent by Growlor.” Min Su answered.
